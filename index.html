About Mainstream Staff Schools

We are delighted to welcome you to mainstream Staff Secondary School, founded in 1971 as NEPA Staff Secondary School, still running and blazing the trail in the educational sector for fifty years +, we continue to do what we do best; giving excellent service to generations and turning out well rounded and proudly Nigerian boys and girls.

What we offer

Highly Effective Teachers

We believe our teachers are the driving force behind our educational methodology. Hence we invest significantly in the continuous professional development of our teachers.

Enriched Curriculum

Our curriculum provides students with diverse learning opportunities focused on broadening their educational experience beyond the conventional curriculum

Extended Learning Programs

Our extended learning programs provide students with increased learning times and guided reading periods, ensuring effective impartation of learning.

A Focus on Character

We believe that academic excellence is not enough to prepare students for the world beyond school, and so we focus on building well-developed character foundations

Conducive Learning Environment

Our serene, nature filled location is devoid of distractions and encour- ages learning while we work to create a safe learning environment

Structured Sports Program

We have a structured sports program that allows students explore and discover their talents and abilities in sports further broadening their future horizons

Our Philosophy

The LEAP-Beyond Approach

LEarning Outcomes

We create in our students a culture of achievement focusing on core skills, learning culture, attainment scores, and environmental safety. We provide an environment that enables our students to thrive and optimize their abilities

Principles and Values

We work to imbibe in students the principles and values that build a strong and resilient character which would underpin their learning

Active learning

We deploy immersive learning experiences that engage students with the curriculum material through discussions, problem-solving, case studies, role plays, tests, stories, solutions, analysis, designs, and other similar methods

BEYOND academics

To develop well-rounded individuals we provide students with programs that go beyond academics Sports, Entrepreneurship, digital literacy, Chess, Public speaking, Competitions, etc.
